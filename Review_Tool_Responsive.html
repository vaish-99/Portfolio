A web tool was developed for users to upload queries and review predictions. Still, low engagement with the tool is delaying the model's deployment and the expected improvements in customer experience..

Experience of Portal Users


Insights


After reviewing the current portal and gathering feedback from its users, I identified several drawbacks that need to be addressed


The Portal's Drawbacks


1. Lack of Connect with the Tool

-Reviewers are unclear on how their review efforts are measured, which reduces their motivation to engage with the tool

-Reviewers feel that the tool unnecessarily consumes additional time


2. Overwhelming Review Data

-The sequential review process leaves reviewers without a clear plan of action, making it difficult to allocate time effectively across all queries

-Reviewers lack a system to identify and prioritize critical queries, leading to inefficiencies in addressing them promptly


3. Lack of flexibility On Queries

-Reviewers lack context on how the model generates its predictions, leading to confusion during the review process

-The current tool does not allow reviewers to assign different statuses, such as "review later" or "peer review," for certain queries, limiting flexibility in managing reviews


4.No proper review system

-Reviewers cannot mark queries as pending for later review, limiting their ability to manage workload efficiently

-There is no system to provide reviewers with insights, such as the number of reviews completed per week or other performance metrics
